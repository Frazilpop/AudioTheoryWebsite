FAQ

When purchasing AudioTheory Guitars (either directly or through Steam) do I get access to both the Mac and PC versions of the software?

Yes.
How do I receive updates for AudioTheory Guitars?

These will be made available for download through Gumroad (for direct purchases) as well as Steam. I will send out email updates with new features so please do subscribe to the AudioTheory newsletter to keep up-to-date.
Is the Mac version of AudioTheory Guitars 64 bit?

Yes – this means it will work on versions of Mac OS that only support 64 bit applications.
